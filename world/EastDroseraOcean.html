East Drosera Ocean

The East Drosera Ocean is an ocean that lies east of Drosera and west of Prosos, Sanguinal, Aquatos, and Petiola. It is considered to be the most treacherous of the four oceans.

Storms occur more frequently in the East Drosera Ocean than any other body of water in Karvaea. It mixes heavily with the much colder Tiptop and South Calla oceans, creating violent systems that threaten the lives of those who dare to sail the dangerous waters.
