Electricity magic

Electricity magic is a type of basic elemental magic. It is also considered a rigid magic. About 7% of bodies in Karvaea have a magical bias towards electricity magic.

There are three primary abilities related to electricity magic:

  • Electrokinesis
  • Telekinesis
  • Rend

Electricity magic is technically not required for any hybrid elemental magic, though when combined with water and wind magic it can help boost the power of storm magic.
