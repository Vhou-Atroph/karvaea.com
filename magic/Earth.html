Earth magic

Earth magic is a type of basic elemental magic. It is also considered a rigid magic. About 15% of bodies in Karvaea have a magical bias towards earth magic.

There are two primary abilities related to earth magic:

  • Geokinesis
  • Crystallokinesis

In addition to its primary uses, earth magic has some application in creating hybrid elemental magic. When combined with fire magic, it can create lava magic. When combined with water magic, it can create nature magic.
