Storm magic

Storm magic is a type of hybrid elemental magic. It is also considered a flowing magic. To create storm magic, two or more bodies must utilize their magical biases of wind and water magic together. Optionally, another body may contribute electricity magic to increase storm magic's power.

There is one primary ability related to storm magic:

  • Tempestakinesis
