Fire magic

Fire magic is a type of basic elemental magic. It is also considered a flowing magic. About 24% of bodies in Karvaea have a magical bias towards fire magic.

There are three primary abilities related to fire magic:

  • Pyrokinesis
  • Fire resistance
  • Hellfire

In addition to its primary uses, fire magic has some application in the creation of hybrid elemental magic. When combined with earth magic, it can create lava magic.
