Neutral magic

Neutral magic is a type of magic that does not fit within the binary of elemental and spiritual magic. It is also considered a rigid magic. Neutral magic is only accessible through runes, but can be used by anyone.

There are a number of potential applications for neutral magic including but not limited to:

  • Traps
  • Object reinforcement
  • Gender affirmation
