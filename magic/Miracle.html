Miracle magic

Miracle magic is a type of spiritual magic. It is also considered a rigid magic. About 2% of bodies in Karvaea have a magical bias towards miracle magic. It is the rarest magical bias.

There are four primary abilities related to miracle magic:

  • Hemokinesis
  • Magical healing
  • Necromancy
  • Smite

Due to its rarity and powerful abilities, there is a certain amount of fear around miracle magic, even among modern society in Karvaea. Even when necessary to use, it is applied sparingly and with great weight.
