Wind magic

Wind magic is a type of basic elemental magic. It is also considered a flowing magic. About 20% of bodies in Karvaea have a magical bias towards wind magic.

There are three primary abilities related to wind magic:

  • Aerokinesis
  • Pseudo telekinesis
  • Thermokinesis

In addition to its primary uses, wind magic has some application in creating hybrid elemental magic. When combined with water and optionally electricity magic, it can create storm magic.
